Tooltips

Tooltips are used to add extra information to an element in the page. This information should not be priority to the user in order to interact with the app properly!

.tooltip.tooltip_right

.tooltip.tooltip_left

.tooltip.tooltip_top

.tooltip.tooltip_bottom

<!-- tooltip tooltip_right -->
<div class="tooltip tooltip_right" value="Here's a tip - drink water."></div>

<!-- tooltip tooltip_left -->
<div class="tooltip tooltip_left" value="How is your day going?"></div>

<!-- tooltip tooltip_top -->
<div class="tooltip tooltip_top" value="Life's greatest treasure is hidden."></div>

<!-- tooltip tooltip_bottom -->
<div class="tooltip tooltip_bottom" value="Arrrrr. Give me your gold dabloons!"></div>
